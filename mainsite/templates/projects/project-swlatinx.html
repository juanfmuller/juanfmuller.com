SWLatinX - Responise wesbite development

MY ROLE

Website Design

Code and Development

CLIENT

Southwest LatinX

DATE

September 2020

Southwest LatinX is a non-porift organization in the El Paso region who helps underpriviliged students have a successful eduaction. As they were expanding they needed a complete overhaul of their website. They were looking for a new design and user experience, which spoke to their authenticity while remaining professional. This included a blog and a way for members to sign up for a news-letter.

I was able to take thier story and develop a website which served many pourposes. It's a way for students to register to events using PayPal, a way to register for a news-letter to recieve the most up to date information, a blog, and finally the front face of the organization.

Working alongside the lead content creator and the director of the organization, we decided to make a website with a simple inerface which could appeal to both students and parents. Formerly, they were using Wix Wesbite Creator, and I moved us to an HTML, CSS, and JS front-end running on a headless CMS for easy edits. I had a lot of creative freedom for this project, however, the hardest part was using the adequate 3rd party services for a manageable website. I ended up utilizing PageClip for the news-letter, PayPal for event registrtaion, Vapid as the CMS, AWS Lightsail for hosting services, and Google Domains for the domain registration.
